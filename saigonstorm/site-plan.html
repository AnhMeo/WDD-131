Overview

Purpose

This site will be a place for fans of the SaiGonStorm Valorant team to learn more about our members, our upcoming match schedule, and to watch some of our matches and highlights. Users will also find links to our social media such as our discord server.

Audience

Fans of SaiGon Storm and team members

Dynamic elements

Homepage: Next match countdown clock Match Archive: Embedded video player with playback controls Filter/sort by date, opponent, or map Schedule: Dynamic calendar of matches, upcoming match that auto-updates based on current date and time
